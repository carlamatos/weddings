A few questions

Good to know

Can I switch themes after I've started?

Yes. Your content stays put — switching themes only changes how it looks, any time, as many times as you like.

Do my guests need an account to RSVP or upload photos?

No. Guests just visit your link. No sign-up, no app, no friction.

What happens to my page after the wedding?

It stays live as a keepsake. Free pages remain viewable indefinitely; Plus pages keep every feature active for as long as you're subscribed.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, with no long-term contract. Cancel whenever you like and your page reverts to the Free plan limits.
